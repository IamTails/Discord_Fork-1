avaire

Add this bot

Prefix: ! (Customizable)

A high quality Music bot, with moderation, interaction, search, administration commands, highly customizable and much more!

So what can it do?
Stream Music

Request music via youtube, soundcloud, twitch, radio links, or by name with ease.

Administration

Administration made easy, take a load off your shoulders and let Ava do the work for you.

Simple AI

You can ask the bot just about anything and it will try and use its AI to answer your questions.

And so much more!
Join & Leave Messages

Broadcast messages when users joins or leaves your server on a per-channel basis.

Chat Slowmode

Prevent people from spamming with slowmode, you can turn it on or off per-channel.

Bilingual Commands

Ava supports multiple languages, you can change what languages Ava uses on the fly.

Change Command Prefixes

Don't like the default prefixes? No problem, you can change them to anything you'd like.

Music Playlists

Create custom music playlists and share your playlists with other people with ease.

Lots of Fun Commands

Ava has a lot of fun/game commands, and more are constantly being added.
