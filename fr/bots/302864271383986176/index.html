Tweetcord

Inviter

Préfixe: t/

A bot that allows you to interact with Twitter

Look no further than Tweetcord for all of your Twitter needs! Tweetcord allows you to interact with your Twitter account. Including, but not limited to:

  • Sending tweets
  • (un)following others
  • A live stream of your timeline with like/retweet functionality at blazing speeds

Tweetcord is still in development. New features and bugfixes are added every day! Keep an eye on https://github.com/aetheryx/tweetcord for updates.
