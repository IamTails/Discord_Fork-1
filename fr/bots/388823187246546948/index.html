Dragon Loli

Inviter Aide

Préfixe: !r

Dragon Loli is a discord bot with fun tools!

This bot is open to requests and changes. To add the commands you always wanted, join our discord server.

Commands:
FUN:

!r urban <word> searches the definition of a word
!r coinflip flips a coin
!r rolldice rolls a dice
!r pat posts an image of a pat
!r avatar <mention> posts the profile picture of the mentioned users (max 3)
!r lewd Sends lewd pics
!r sleep Sends picture of Kanna sleeping

Mod:

!r clean removes my latest messages and user-made bot messages
– requires Dragon Mod role –
!r kick notinside <role> kicks all members that are not inside that role

Information:

!r help for help
!r changelog to see new changes
!r invitebot for the invite link
!r support for the bot server link
